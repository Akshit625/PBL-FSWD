About RoomRadar

Helping students find housing with less confusion

RoomRadar is a student-focused housing platform built to make nearby room discovery simple, clear, and fast. Students can explore listings near colleges, compare options, and contact owners directly without jumping across multiple websites.

What We Do

We bring student housing search, listing details, and owner contact into one easy interface.

Our goal is to reduce the time students spend searching and help owners present their properties more clearly.

Why It Matters

Finding accommodation near campus is often stressful, especially for students moving to a new city. RoomRadar keeps the process straightforward with focused filters, simple listings, and direct communication.

Who It's For

Students Search nearby rooms, flats, hostels, and PGs with less effort.
Property owners Create listings, manage details, and respond to inquiries from interested students.
Campus communities Support easier access to housing options around colleges and universities.
